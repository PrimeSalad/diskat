# Plan Mode: Project Diskat 🌸

## Executive Summary
**Diskat** is a personalized, single-page digital gift. Instead of a complex app, it is a high-effort, visually stunning "Digital Love Letter" and memory vault. It focuses on storytelling, custom animations, and a polished aesthetic to show genuine effort.

---

## 🎨 Aesthetic & Design (The "Effort")
- **Fonts:** 
  - `Poppins`: For bold, meaningful headlines.
  - `Questrial`: For intimate, handwritten-style notes and body text.
- **Theme:** "Soft Minimalism" – using warm creams, dusty roses, and deep charcoals.
- **Animations:** 
  - Parallax scrolling for memories.
  - Floating hearts/sparkles that follow the mouse.
  - Reveal-on-scroll text for a "storytelling" feel.

---

## 🛠️ Key Sections
### 1. The Hero: "To My Favorite Person"
- A beautiful entrance animation.
- A personalized greeting that fades in.

### 2. The Timeline: "Our Journey"
- A vertical or horizontal scroll of key dates/memories.
- High-quality placeholders for photos with custom frames.

### 3. The Interactive Letter
- A digital envelope that "opens" when clicked.
- A long-form message written in Questrial.

### 4. The "Small Things" Grid
- A grid of small interactive cards. Hovering reveals "small things" you love about her.

---

## 📅 Roadmap
1. **Scaffold:** Clean Next.js template (Static Export).
2. **Typography:** Set up the Poppins/Questrial pairing.
3. **Sections:** Build the Hero, Timeline, and Letter components.
4. **Polish:** Add Framer Motion for every single transition.
5. **Deploy:** One-click Vercel deployment.

---

## 💎 Why this is "Effort"
- **Not a Template:** Custom-coded layouts and unique interactive elements.
- **Hand-picked Details:** Small "Easter Eggs" like a heart popping when she clicks a certain word.
- **Privacy:** 100% Static, no data collection, just a pure gift.
